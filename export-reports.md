# How to Export Reports

## Overview

TeamFlow allows users to export project and productivity reports for external analysis.

## Steps

1. Open the **Reports Dashboard**.
2. Select the report you want to export.
3. Click **Export**.
4. Choose a file format.
5. Click **Download**.

## Supported Formats

* PDF
* CSV
* Excel

## Troubleshooting

If the export fails, verify that you have permission to access reporting features.
