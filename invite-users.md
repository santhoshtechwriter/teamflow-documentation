# How to Invite Users

## Overview

Workspace administrators can invite users to collaborate within TeamFlow.

## Steps

1. Open your workspace.
2. Navigate to **Members**.
3. Click **Invite Member**.
4. Enter the user's email address.
5. Select a role.
6. Click **Send Invitation**.

## Available Roles

* Owner
* Administrator
* Member
* Guest

## Troubleshooting

### Invitation Not Received

* Verify the email address.
* Check spam folders.
* Resend the invitation.
