# How to Reset Your Password

## Overview

If you have forgotten your password or need to update your account credentials, TeamFlow allows you to reset your password securely.

## Reset Password from the Login Page

1. Open the TeamFlow login page.
2. Click **Forgot Password**.
3. Enter your registered email address.
4. Click **Send Reset Link**.

A password reset email will be sent to your inbox.

## Create a New Password

1. Open the password reset email.
2. Click the reset link.
3. Enter a new password.
4. Confirm the password.
5. Click **Save Password**.

Your new password will be activated immediately.

## Password Requirements

Passwords must contain:

* At least 8 characters
* One uppercase letter
* One lowercase letter
* One number

## Troubleshooting

### Reset Email Not Received

Possible causes:

* Incorrect email address
* Spam filtering
* Email delivery delays

Resolution:

1. Verify the email address entered.
2. Check your spam folder.
3. Wait a few minutes and try again.
4. Contact support if the issue persists.
