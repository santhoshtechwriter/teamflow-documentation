# Getting Started with TeamFlow

## Overview

TeamFlow is a cloud-based project management platform that helps teams organize projects, manage tasks, collaborate with team members, and track progress in real time.

This guide walks you through the initial setup process.

## Prerequisites

Before getting started, ensure that you have:

* A valid email address
* Internet access
* An invitation from your organization or an active TeamFlow subscription

## Create an Account

Follow these steps to create your TeamFlow account:

1. Navigate to the TeamFlow sign-up page.
2. Enter your name, email address, and password.
3. Click **Create Account**.
4. Verify your email address using the verification link.
5. Sign in to TeamFlow.

## Create Your First Workspace

A workspace is the primary area where projects, users, and settings are managed.

To create a workspace:

1. Sign in to TeamFlow.
2. Click **Create Workspace**.
3. Enter a workspace name.
4. Select your preferred workspace settings.
5. Click **Save**.

## Invite Team Members

You can collaborate with others by inviting team members.

To invite users:

1. Open your workspace.
2. Navigate to **Members**.
3. Click **Invite Members**.
4. Enter the user's email address.
5. Select a role.
6. Click **Send Invitation**.

## Create Your First Project

Projects help organize work into manageable units.

To create a project:

1. Open your workspace.
2. Click **New Project**.
3. Enter a project name.
4. Add a description.
5. Click **Create Project**.

## Next Steps

After creating your first project, you can:

* Create tasks
* Assign team members
* Set deadlines
* Monitor project progress
* Generate reports

For additional guidance, refer to the TeamFlow Knowledge Base.
